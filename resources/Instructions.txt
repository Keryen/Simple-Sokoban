Welcome to Sokoban!
The goal of this game is to bring all boxes to the goal points.

In order to play the game, there are some things you need to take into account:
1. You cannot walk through walls.
2. You cannot push boxes into walls.
3. Only one box fits on one goal point.
4. Both pushing and moving actions count 1 in the level score.
5. You can undo actions or reset the level.
6. You can save and load games anytime.
7. The level finishes when all boxes are on goal positions and the game finishes when there are no more levels left to play.

Enjoy and good luck!
